ChatGPT for Support

Introduction

This is a test page to evaluate the cababilities of chatGPT for supporting Holded users

Test Instructions

To test the bot, follow these steps:

  1. Click on the chat widget in the bottom right corner of the screen to open the chat.
  2. Send an example question to the chatbot, such as "What services do you provide?" or "How can I contact support?"
  3. Wait for the chatbot to respond and evaluate its answer.
  4. If needed, click the "Reset Chatbot" button to clear the conversation and test with another question.
